Everything you need to know about TripKawan.

TripKawan is a group travel planning app designed for friends and families in Malaysia and ASEAN. It brings collaborative itinerary planning and automatic expense splitting into one simple, stress-free tool — no more juggling WhatsApp, spreadsheets, and Notes apps.

Log any expense during your trip — meals, transport, accommodation — and TripKawan automatically calculates who owes what. At the end of the trip you get a simple settlement summary so everyone knows exactly what to pay back.

Yes — TripKawan is completely free during our early access phase. We're collecting feedback from real travelers to build the best possible product before launch.

TripKawan is built for small friend groups, families, and colleagues in Malaysia and across ASEAN who travel together and want a stress-free way to plan itineraries and split trip costs without awkward money conversations.
